Offline-first & resilient

It never loses your work — online or off.

Network drops, the server's down for maintenance, you're working remote — the app stays fully navigable, cached clips stay playable, and every edit is queued in a durable journal that flushes when you reconnect.

01

Three-layer media cache

Proxies are served from local cache, so cached clips stay playable even with no network at all.

02

Graceful degradation

Login fails or a probe drops? The app degrades to offline automatically — one click to reconnect.

03

Durable write queue

Edits made offline are journaled and flushed in order when you're back — nothing lost, nothing doubled.

Runs where you need it

Same app. Three places.

01 · HOST

On the archive host

Zero-copy proxy reads straight from the media-store — no download, no second cache.

02 · LAPTOP

On a laptop

Behind your VPN, working remote. Degrades to offline automatically when the network drops.

03 · CLOUD

In the cloud

Deploy to Cloud Run when you want it always-on and reachable by the whole team.
